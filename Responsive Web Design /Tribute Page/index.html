Who is Elon Musk?
  • 1971 Elon Reeve Musk was born in Pretoria, Transvaal, South Africa
  • 1995 Co-founded online city guide software company Zip2
  • 2002 Founded SpaceX, a spaceflight services company
  • 2004 Started investing in electric vehicle manufacturer Tesla Motors
  • 2008 Became the CEO of Tesla Motors
  • 2013 Proposed a hyperloop high-speed vactrain transportation system
  • 2015 Co-founded OpenAI
  • 2022 Acquired Twitter for $44 billion, merged the company into newly created X Corp.
